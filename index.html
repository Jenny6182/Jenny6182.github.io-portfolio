About Me

Hi! I’m Jenny — a software developer, physics undergrad researcher, and a University of Toronto Aerospace Team (UTAT) space systems lead passionate about bridging science and technology. I lead the ADCS team of 26 members on FINCH, the second CubeSat developed by UTAT, the team behind Canada’s first fully student-built and student-funded satellite. With a background in physics and math, I develop algorithms from first principles and bring them to life through code. I’ve applied this in spectral unmixing research with the Science team and in complex system simulations with COBWEB, exploring how computation can drive discovery and innovation. I’m fascinated by quantum physics, space systems, and how we can use software to turn abstract concepts into real, testable technology.
